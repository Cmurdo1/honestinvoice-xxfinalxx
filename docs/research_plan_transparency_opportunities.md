# Research Plan: Transparency and Trust Opportunities for HonestInvoice.com

## Task Overview
Identify specific opportunities for HonestInvoice.com to differentiate through transparency and trust features based on competitor analysis and market pain points.

## Research Objectives
1. [ ] Common billing disputes and their root causes
2. [ ] Lack of transparency in traditional invoicing systems  
3. [ ] Trust building features missing in current invoicing tools
4. [ ] Blockchain/trust verification technologies applicable to invoicing
5. [ ] Competitive landscape analysis of existing invoicing solutions
6. [ ] Client relationship pain points in billing processes

## Research Strategy
- **Phase 1**: Web research on billing disputes and invoicing challenges
- **Phase 2**: Competitor analysis of major invoicing platforms
- **Phase 3**: Investigation of blockchain/trust technologies in fintech
- **Phase 4**: Synthesis and opportunity identification

## Information Sources Strategy
- Industry reports and surveys
- Competitor websites and documentation
- Academic papers on billing disputes
- Fintech and blockchain research
- User reviews and feedback
- Professional forums and discussions

## Expected Deliverables
- Comprehensive analysis saved to `research/transparency_opportunities.md`
- Source tracking for all findings
- Actionable differentiation opportunities

## Timeline
- Research Phase: 1-2 hours
- Analysis and Report Generation: 30 minutes
