# Invoice Management Platforms Research Plan

## Objective
Research major invoice management platforms (QuickBooks, FreshBooks, Invoice Ninja, Zoho Invoice, Wave, Xero) to identify their strengths, weaknesses, and market gaps, with specific focus on transparency, trust, and client relationships.

## Research Focus Areas
1. **Core invoice features** - Invoice creation, customization, automation, recurring invoices, templates
2. **Transparency** - Pricing structures, fee disclosure, terms clarity, data usage policies
3. **Trust & Security** - Payment security, compliance, fraud protection, data encryption, certifications
4. **Client Relationships** - Client portals, communication tools, payment experiences, dispute handling
5. **Market Positioning** - Target market, competitive advantages, unique features
6. **User Experience** - Interface design, ease of use, customer support quality
